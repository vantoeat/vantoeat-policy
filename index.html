VanToEat Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 29, 2026

1. Information We Collect

Location Data: VanToEat requests access to your device's GPS location to find restaurants near you. This data is processed in real-time to query the Google Places API and is NOT stored on our servers.

Device Identifiers: We use Google AdMob to serve advertisements. AdMob may collect your device's advertising ID to provide personalized ads.

2. How We Use Information

  • To provide the "Spin" and "Explore" restaurant discovery functions.
  • To facilitate navigation via third-party map applications (Apple/Google Maps).

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Location coordinates are shared with the Google Places API solely to retrieve restaurant results.
